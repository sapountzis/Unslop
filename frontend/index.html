FAQ

Answering the real concerns before you install.

Is this safe? ▾
Yes. We do not touch your LinkedIn account data. Unslop works in your browser and adjusts how posts render in your feed.
Will this break LinkedIn? ▾
It is designed to be lightweight and non-destructive. You can disable Unslop anytime and LinkedIn returns to normal behavior.
Does it "block" people? ▾
No. It does not block accounts. You can still view dimmed or hidden posts; Unslop just lowers the volume so you do not have to engage.
Are you selling my data? ▾
No tracking pixels. No ad analytics. No data resale. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.
Why is it called "Slop"? ▾
Because you deserve a diet of high-quality ideas, not junk food for your attention span.
