FAQ

Quick answers. No fluff.

Does it “block” LinkedIn content permanently? ▾
No, it simply changes how posts render in your feed (keep, dim, or hide). You stay in control and can turn it off anytime.
Will this slow down LinkedIn? ▾
It’s designed to be lightweight. Decisions can be cached so the same posts don’t re-process.
