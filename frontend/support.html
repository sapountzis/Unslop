Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign in?

Click the Unslop extension icon in Chrome → “Sign In” → enter your email → click the magic link.

The sign-in link expires in 15 minutes. If it expires, request a new one.

Why was a post hidden?

Unslop classifies posts as keep or hide. Posts that look like:

  • Engagement bait (“Like if you agree!”)
  • Vague platitudes without substance
  • Spam or scams

may be hidden. The classifier isn’t perfect — you can send feedback.

What are the usage limits?

Free plan: 300 AI classifications per month (cached posts don’t count)

Pro plan: 10,000 AI classifications per month

After you exceed your limit, posts are shown normally until your quota resets.

How do I upgrade to Pro?

Open the extension popup → “Upgrade to Pro”. You’ll be taken to checkout.

How do I cancel?

Cancel via your billing portal, or email support@getunslop.com. Your account returns to Free at period end.

The extension isn’t working

  • Confirm you’re on linkedin.com (desktop site)
  • Ensure the extension is enabled in Chrome
  • Make sure filtering is toggled ON in the popup

If it still fails, email support with a short description + screenshot.
